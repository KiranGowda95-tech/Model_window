About the KGF movie description

K.G.F: Chapter 1 is a 2018 Indian Kannada-language period action film written and directed by Prashanth Neel, and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under the banner Hombale films. ... The film features Yash in the lead role as Rocky, while debutante Ramachandra Raju features as Garuda.
